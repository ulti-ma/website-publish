About Me

My name is Htoo and I study Digital Graphics at Saint Paul College in St. Paul, Minnesota. My planned graduation date is May 2025 with the Visualization Technology AAS. I made this website as a portfolio of my skills developing websites.

I have taken the following classes at Saint Paul College:

  • Web Fundamentals, where I learned HTML and CSS Computer Science and Information Systems Introduction to Computing and Programming Concepts.
  • Introduction to Computer Graphics where we were introduced to a variety of software applications used in visualiztion technology.
  • Adobe Animate 1 & 2 where we covered Adobe Animate and were introduced to animation and animation principles.
  • Digital Video 1 where we covered video production, and learned technqiues involving multitrack video editing along with digital audio and creation of various titles and effects.


I am also planning on taking more courses with an animation emphasis toward my Visualization Technology AAS Degree. My hobbies include video gaming, drawing, and running.
